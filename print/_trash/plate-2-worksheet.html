Penetrating Questions
Very Short Answers
1
What makes "East Asia" a coherent region, not just geography?
Shared writing (漢字), Confucian values, Buddhist practice, governmental models — but each country adapted locally
2
How did Chinese characters both unite AND divide East Asian societies?
United elites across borders; divided elite from commoners (years to learn) → eventually led to Hangul, kana
