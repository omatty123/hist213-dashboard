Socialist Realism FINAL PROJECT

State-sanctioned art glorifying communist values through idealized workers, peasants, and revolutionary heroes. Dominant in USSR, China, and North Korea from 1930s-1980s. Modern echoes persist in North Korean media and some Chinese cinema.

WEIRD Bias in Psychology FINAL PROJECT

Western, Educated, Industrialized, Rich, Democratic — 12% of humanity, yet 96% of psychology research subjects. East Asian societies show different patterns in self-concept, social cognition, and decision-making that challenge "universal" findings.

Topic 8

The Opium Wars: Visual Archive FINAL PROJECT

MIT's Visualizing Cultures project offers an extraordinary visual archive of the First Opium War. Sherwill's lithographs show industrial-scale opium production at Patna. Shiva Lal's paintings humanize Indian workers in the same factory. The Nemesis — first iron warship ever built — destroyed Chinese junks.

Topic 9

Dejima & Tokugawa "Foreigners" FINAL PROJECT

A 120m x 75m artificial island in Nagasaki harbor — Japan's only official window to the West for 200 years (1641-1853). Dutch traders lived under strict surveillance. How did Tokugawa artists portray these "exotic" visitors? Kawahara Keiga's paintings offer a fascinating lens on cross-cultural perception.
