Scholars' Definitions
Thinker Definition / Key Idea Agree?
Max Weber Modernity = rationalization, bureaucracy, disenchantment of the world. The "iron cage" of efficiency.
Karl Marx Modernity = capitalism, class conflict, industrial production. Europe leads because of its mode of production.
Andre G. Frank Modernity is a myth. Asia was the center until 1800. Europe didn't "create" modernity — it climbed on Asia's back.
Enlightenment View Modernity = progress, reason, science, individual rights. Began in Europe, spread outward.
Postcolonial Critique "Modernity" is code for "European." It's a power move disguised as a timeline.
