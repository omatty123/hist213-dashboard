Penetrating Questions Very short answers
1 Was the occupation democratization or colonization? Americans imposed reforms, but those reforms were genuinely democratic. Japanese had no choice—but many welcomed changes. The constitution was American-drafted but Japanese-ratified. Both/and rather than either/or.
2 Why was Article 9 included, and what does it mean today? Americans wanted to prevent Japanese rearmament. Many Japanese embraced pacifism after war's horrors. But Cold War led to Self-Defense Forces. Debates continue: revise Article 9? How interpret it?
