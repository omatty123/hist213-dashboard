Penetrating Questions Very short answers
1 What were the human and environmental costs of Japan's economic miracle? Minamata, air pollution, overwork, gender inequality. These weren't accidents but structural features of the development model. Who paid the price for Japan's success?
2 How do social movements challenge dominant narratives of 'success'? Environmental and feminist activists told different stories than the 'Japan as Number One' narrative. Whose stories get told? Whose get suppressed? This connects to course themes about Eurocentrism.
